Nepalese Student Association (NSA) at ISU

NSA is a happy and proud community comprised of Nepalese students studying at Idaho State university along with other Nepalese people staying in Pocatello Idaho.

What we do?

Organize different recreational, festive and cultural events throuhout the year, including Nepalese Night, Dashain, Tihar, Holi, Welcome to New Arrivals and many other sports events.

Members

There over 290 members in NSA as of 2018 Janaury 13, including over 190 students at ISU and rest are other Nepalese people living in Pocatello, Idaho.
